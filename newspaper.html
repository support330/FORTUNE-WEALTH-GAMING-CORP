REVOLUTION HOLD'EM RELEASED

By FWGC | Jul 25, 2025

Fortune Wealth Gaming Corp. Officially Releases Revolution!

“Fortune Wealth Gaming Corp. is proud to announce the official release of its latest product, Revolution, a global web-based gaming platform designed to bring players a secure, fair, and engaging online experience.”

At launch, Revolution offers Texas Hold’em cash games and tournaments, already drawing more than 3,000 players daily with around 500 players active simultaneously. The platform features weekly and weekend tournaments with prize pools of up to $2,400, alongside a 4.5% rake model with a 0.5% rakeback system, ensuring players receive added value.

Revolution is built on a points-based ecosystem (1 USD = 1,000 points) with seamless transactions, rapid processing, and strong support for both players and partner agents

  • Backed by RNG certification, the platform guarantees transparency and fairness at every stage of gameplay– ensuring trust and integrity for players worldwide.
  • Exciting weekly and weekend tournaments – offering prize pools of up to $2,400 with structured payouts.
  • Rewarding rakeback system – 0.5% of rake returned to players as points with real monetary value.
  • Upcoming game releases – Omaha, Niu Niu, Slots, and Blockchain-based Baccarat will soon join the platform
