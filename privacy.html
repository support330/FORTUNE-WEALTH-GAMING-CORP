Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy is our top priority. Learn how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Policy Statement

The company values your interest, patronage, and privacy. We have implemented policies that govern the collection, use, storage, and protection of your personal data in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

This Privacy Notice summarizes our practices and applies to all services and websites owned or directly operated by the company.

Scope and Consent

This Privacy Notice applies to all personal data processed manually or through automated systems. By using our services, you consent to the collection, use, disclosure, and protection of your personal data.

If you submit personal data on behalf of another person, you confirm you are authorized to do so. If you choose not to provide your information, we may not be able to deliver the services you request.

Your Rights and Preferences

Under the Data Privacy Act, you have the following rights:

1. The right to be informed about how your personal data is collected and used.
2. The right to access and rectify your personal data.
3. The right to withdraw consent, object to processing, or request deletion.
4. The right to file complaints and seek indemnity for damages, if applicable.

How We Collect Your Information

In compliance with PAGCOR regulations and to provide you with better services, we collect data through our website, call centers, and on-site interactions.
