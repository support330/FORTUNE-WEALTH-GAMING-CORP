Partnership Announcement

By FWGC | Jul 25, 2025

Fortune Wealth Gaming Corp. is proud to announce its commitment to forging strong industry collaborations through its latest product, Revolution.

“We are actively seeking partnerships with industry leaders to integrate Revolution Hold’em into their platforms, bringing world-class online poker experiences to a wider global audience.”

With more than 3,000 daily players and backed by RNG certification, Revolution is designed to deliver fairness, scalability, and a rewarding gameplay model. Through strategic partnerships, Fortune Wealth Gaming Corp. aims to expand Revolution’s reach while offering secure and seamless integration for platform providers.

Benefits of Partnering with FWGC for Revolution Hold’em

  • Seamless Integration – Revolution Hold’em can be integrated smoothly into existing platforms.
  • Growing Player Base – Access to over 3,000+ active players daily.
  • Proven Revenue Model – Rake and rakeback systems designed for sustainable returns.
  • Trusted by RNG Certification – Guaranteeing fairness and transparency in every game.

Join Us in Shaping the Future of Online Gaming
